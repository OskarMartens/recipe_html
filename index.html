This is my favorite pancake recipe. This is the recipe my grandmother usually makes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 dl of flour.
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt.
  • 6 dl of milk.
  • 3 eggs.
  • Butter for frying.
  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add half of the milk and whisk until a smooth batter.
  3. Then add the rest of the milk and the eggs.
  4. Fry the pancakes in some butter in a frying pan.
  5. Serve the pancakes with some jam, I prefer strawberry.
